About CBE

CBE - Consultancy in Burmese and English was founded with the mission to bridge the communication gap between Burmese and English speakers worldwide. We are passionate about language, culture, and helping our clients achieve their goals through clear and accurate communication.

  • Expertise: Our team consists of native Burmese and English linguists with years of experience in translation, interpretation, and localization.
  • Approach: We combine linguistic expertise with cultural understanding to ensure your message resonates with your target audience.
  • Clients: We serve businesses, non-profits, academic institutions, and individuals who need high-quality language services they can trust.

At CBE, we believe that language is more than just words—it’s about connection, understanding, and opportunity. Whether you need document translation, live interpretation for important meetings, or localization for your website or app, we’re here to help you succeed.
